Model & Feature Notes

Raw features: rpm, current, torque, dc_voltage, temperature, vibration. Derived at engineering time: motor_on (rpm > 5), mechanical_power_proxy (|torque|×rpm), torque_current_ratio, plus rolling-window statistics (mean, std, rate-of-change over 10 readings). DC bus voltage reflects VFD/inverter-fed drive — power is derived as dc_voltage × current and labeled accordingly. Torque sign indicates regenerative (braking) vs. drive operation.
